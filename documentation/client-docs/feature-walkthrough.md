# Feature Walkthrough

Welcome to the feature walkthrough! Here is a simple, beginner-friendly guide to understanding all 9 modules of the platform.

---

### 1. GMB Audit Engine
**What it is:** Your SEO X-Ray machine.
**How it works:** You type in your business name, and the platform queries live Google Maps data. Our AI analyzes your photos, reviews, and profile completeness to give you actionable scores (e.g., SEO, Engagement). It instantly tells you exactly what you need to fix to rank higher.

### 2. AI Content Generator
**What it is:** Your personal copywriter.
**How it works:** Tell the platform your tone (Professional, Friendly) and the type of post you want (Promotional, Educational, FAQ). The AI instantly drafts a high-quality Google Business post complete with local SEO keywords, hashtags, and a call-to-action.

### 3. Scheduler & Auto-Posting
**What it is:** Your content calendar on autopilot.
**How it works:** We maintain a "7-Day Buffer". Every day, our background workers check your calendar. If you have less than 7 days of posts scheduled, the AI automatically drafts new, unique posts to fill the gap. It then publishes them to Google exactly on time.

### 4. Review Management Agent
**What it is:** Your reputation defender.
**How it works:** The platform pulls in all your Google reviews. The AI analyzes the sentiment (Positive, Negative, Critical) and instantly drafts a polite, contextual reply. For critical 1-star reviews, it will instantly text the business owner so you can jump in and save the relationship.

### 5. CRM System
**What it is:** Your lead command center.
**How it works:** A visual, drag-and-drop Kanban board where you can see every single lead. Leads move through stages: New → Contacted → Qualified → Interested → Booking Pending → Converted.

### 6. WhatsApp AI Agent
**What it is:** Your 24/7 receptionist.
**How it works:** When a customer texts your business WhatsApp number, the AI replies instantly. It answers questions, asks for their requirements, and figures out their budget. It then saves all this info directly into the CRM System and scores their "Intent to Buy".

### 7. Automation Engine
**What it is:** The invisible brain.
**How it works:** Built on Inngest, this system runs in the background. It ensures that cron jobs fire every 15 minutes, retries failed messages if the network drops, and orchestrates delays (like waiting 2 days before sending a follow-up text).

### 8. Admin Dashboard
**What it is:** Your control panel.
**How it works:** A beautiful, dark-mode, glassmorphism UI where you control the entire platform. It unites all modules into one seamless experience.

### 9. Review Generation System
**What it is:** Your 5-star review printer.
**How it works:** Upload a CSV list of your past customers. The platform automatically sends them a friendly SMS asking for a review. If they don't click the link, it waits 2 days and sends a gentle reminder. We track exactly who clicked and who actually left a review.
